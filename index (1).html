Free Fire Tips and Tricks

1. Master Your Sensitivity Settings

Adjust your sensitivity settings to suit your play style. This helps in fast reactions and accurate shots.

In Free Fire, aiming accurately is crucial. Practice with different weapons and find your perfect aim.
